## Why mornings matter

The first 30 minutes of your day set the tone for everything that follows. A chaotic morning creates a chaotic mind. A calm morning creates intention.

## The 10-minute framework

**Minutes 1–3: No phone.** Sit with your coffee. Look out a window. Let your brain wake up naturally.

**Minutes 4–6: Three deep breaths.** Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4, exhale for 6. Repeat three times.

**Minutes 7–9: One intention.** What's the one thing that would make today feel worthwhile? Write it down.

**Minute 10: Move.** Ten squats, ten push-ups, ten jumping jacks. Just enough to get blood moving.

## The key is consistency

Don't try to meditate for 45 minutes if you've never done it. Start with ten minutes. Do it every day. Build from there.
