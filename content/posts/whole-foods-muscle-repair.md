## Recovery starts in the kitchen

You can train perfectly and still leave gains on the table if your nutrition doesn't support repair. Here are five foods that work while you rest.

## 1. Tart cherry juice

Rich in anthocyanins that reduce inflammation and speed up muscle recovery. A small glass before bed is a game changer.

## 2. Cottage cheese

Slow-digesting casein protein feeds your muscles throughout the night. Add some honey and you have a perfect pre-sleep snack.

## 3. Kiwi

Two kiwis before bed have been shown to improve sleep quality — and better sleep means better recovery, full stop.

## 4. Eggs

Complete amino acid profile. The yolk contains leucine, a key trigger for muscle protein synthesis.

## 5. Walnuts

A natural source of melatonin and omega-3s. A handful in the evening supports both sleep and inflammation reduction.
